I am currently a PhD student of CBE at HKUST.I once carried out my graduation project in the Research Group of Molecular Simulation and Modeling, and studied under professors Liu Yingchun and Wang Qi with the research topic Study on the Transport Mechanism of Lithium Ions in Sulfone Based Electrolyte of Lithium Ion Battery.

I am deeply passionate about the intricate world of molecular simulation within the realm of computational simulation and theoretical modeling of polymers and soft matter. My focus lies in the bioengineering applications of these advanced techniques, where I strive to unravel the mysteries of material behavior at the molecular level. I am committed to contributing innovative solutions that can drive progress in this dynamic field.
